Mandate Summary

Every buyer and seller listed in the marketplace begins their engagement with a standardized AI mandate. This mandate outlines the scope of work, milestone checkpoints, deliverables, and dispute resolution terms to ensure trustless transactions.

  • Participants: Buyer, Seller, AI Escrow Agent
  • Escrow Type: Tokenized smart contract (multi-sig)
  • Verification: On-chain proof of funds and identity validation
  • Completion Criteria: Milestones approved by both parties or automated testing suites
